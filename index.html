⚙️ Control Panel

Cashback (CB)

Cashback is credited daily at 09:00 CET and is calculated based on net real-money loss from the previous day. You receive based on your level up to 25% cashback.

Net Loss Formula & Rules

Net Loss Formula

(Previous Day): Total Deposits – Total Withdrawals – Current Balance

Rules

  • Min. CB: 3€
  • Max. CB: 25% of daily deposits
  • Max. win: credited CB ×10
  • Wager: credited CB ×3
    (From level 10 and above, cashback is fully wager-free.)
  • Top-Up bonuses are excluded from cashback calculation.

Top-Up Bonus

From Starlord level onward, you receive an automatic Top-Up boost on every deposit, increasing the amount you receive based on your status — between 1% and 5%.

How it works & Example
  • Consul (+1%), Baron (+2%), Duke (+3%), Regent (+4%), Sovereign (+5%)
  • No activation needed — the boost is automatic.
  • Comes with a minimal 1× wagering requirement.

Example

At Sovereign status, depositing 1,000€ gives you an extra 50€ Cash Bonus instantly.

Wager Points (WP)

The Fuel for Your Race. WP determine your status in the loyalty program. The more you collect, the higher your permanent cashback percentage.

How to Earn WPs
  • Slots: 1 WP per €50 wagered (≈ 5€ deposit)
  • Other Games: 1 WP per €100 wagered (≈ 10€ deposit)

Deposit Points (DP)

The Keys to the VIP Universe. DP are required to advance to the Frontier tier and beyond, in addition to Wager Points. The rate is simple: €10 Deposit = 1 DP.

Star Coins (SC)

The Victor's Currency. You get them for completing missions. Spend them in the Star Shop on free spins and other trophies. They do not affect your level progression.

Star Shop

The Star Shop is our exclusive marketplace where you can exchange your accumulated Star Coins (SC) for rewards of your choice, such as Free Spins or Bonuses.

💬 FAQ Section
How do I join The Star Race?

You automatically become a member of The Star Race as soon as you make your first real money deposit at Bet4Star. No extra steps or fees are required. Welcome to the race!

Is it free?

Absolutely! Membership is completely free and automatic for all our players. Your journey begins as soon as you start playing.

What is a Tier?

Tiers (or Acts) are the main stages of your journey. Think of them as broad categories — Beginner, Frontier, Starlord — that group several levels together.

Moving to a new tier unlocks major benefits. Progressing through levels inside a tier gives you incremental rewards.

Where can I see my current status and progress?

You can view your Star Race status — including current level, Wager Points (WP) balance or Deposit Points (DP) balance, and upgrade progress — directly in your personal account dashboard.

A dynamic progress bar shows how close you are to the next level, based on both your WP and DP history. It updates in real time and tells you exactly what’s still needed to unlock the next reward tier.

Will I lose my status if I'm inactive?

Never. Your status in The Star Race is permanent and based on your total lifetime accumulation of Wager Points (WP). You can never be demoted, so you can always take a break and pick up right where you left off.

What are missions and challenges?

Missions are personalized challenges designed to enhance your gameplay experience. Completing missions earns you rewards — like extra Star Coins (SC), Free Spins, or cash bonuses. Check your account for active missions!

What is the Star Shop?

The Star Shop is our exclusive marketplace where you can directly exchange your accumulated Star Coins (SC) for rewards of your choice. This gives you full control over how you use your loyalty points. Check the shop in your account for the latest inventory!

What's the difference between 'Wager' and 'Deposit'?

A deposit is the money you transfer into your Bet4Star account. Wager (or turnover) is the total amount of money you bet in games. For example, if you deposit 50€ and place 50 bets of 1€, your wager is 50€ — regardless of wins or losses. You earn WPs based on your total wager.

How is my daily Cashback calculated?

Cashback is calculated daily based on your real money losses from the previous day (00:00 to 23:59 CET). The formula is: (Total Deposits - Total Withdrawals - Current Real Money Balance). The resulting amount is multiplied by your current cashback percentage.

What is 'Wager-free Cashback'?

Wager-free cashback is pure cash! Starting from Level 10 (Captain), the cashback you receive has no wagering requirements. You can play with it immediately or withdraw it, giving you complete freedom.

How does the Top-Up Bonus work?

The Top-Up Bonus is an exclusive perk for our Starlord tier members. It's an extra percentage of cash added directly to your deposit. With a 5% Top-Up bonus, a 100€ deposit becomes 105€ in your balance. The extra 5€ only has a 1× wager.

How do I get a personal VIP manager?

You will be assigned a personal VIP manager as soon as you reach Level 8 (Pilot) in the Frontier tier. They will contact you to introduce themselves and will be your primary point of contact for a truly premium experience.

How can I receive real Gifts & Event invitations?

Once you reach a status that offers these elite perks (Level 14+), your personal VIP manager will contact you. They will discuss your preferences to ensure gifts are tailored to you and will send personal invitations to exclusive events.

Are Top-Up bonuses and withdrawal limits cumulative across Starlord levels?

No — bonuses like Top-Up % and withdrawal limit increases are replaced as you progress through Starlord levels, not stacked.

For example, if you're at Level 15 (Regent) with a +4% Top-Up bonus, that completely replaces the benefits from Level 12–14. You always receive the strongest active benefits from your current status.
